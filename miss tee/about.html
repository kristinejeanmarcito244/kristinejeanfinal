About Education



EDUCATION- Discipline that is concerned with methods of teaching and learning in school
like environments as opposed to various nonformal and informal means of socialization (e,g.,
development projects and education through parent-child relationships).

Education- is a broad concept that encompasses the process of teaching and learning typically in a formal setting
such as a school or college.It involves the acquisition of knowledge, skills, and understanding through various methods and theories of teaching.
The exact definition of education may vary depending on different contexts and perspectives.

According to the Cambridge Dictionary, education refers to the process of teaching or learning, as well as the knowledge
gained from this process. It can also involve the study of teaching methods and theories.
